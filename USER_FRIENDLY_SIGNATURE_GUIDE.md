# 👤 User-Friendly E-Signature Guide

## 🎯 For Non-IT Users: How to Get Signature Access

### Step 1: Request Authorization (Self-Service) 🙋‍♂️

1. **Login to the system** with your username and password
2. **Click "Request Signature Access"** in the left sidebar menu
3. **Fill out the simple form**:
   - Select your name from the dropdown (e.g., "O.M. LAVA")
   - Choose your role (e.g., "Prepared by", "Approved by")
   - Explain why you need access (e.g., "I prepare daily reports for Agus plants")
4. **Click "Submit Request"**
5. **You're done!** ✅ You'll get an email when it's approved

### Step 2: Wait for Approval 📧

- **Admin gets notified** automatically
- **Typical approval time**: 1-2 business days
- **You'll receive an email** when approved or if more info is needed

### Step 3: Create Your Digital Signature ✍️

Once approved:
1. **Go to "Generate Report"** page
2. **Click "e-signature"** button next to your name
3. **Draw your signature** using your mouse or touchscreen
4. **Save it** - it's now stored securely

### Step 4: Sign Reports with Security 🔐

When signing reports:
1. **Click "e-signature"** next to your name
2. **Check your email** for a 6-digit code
3. **Enter the code** in the system
4. **Your signature is applied** with full security

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why do I need authorization?
**A:** For security! Only authorized personnel can sign official reports. This prevents unauthorized signatures and ensures accountability.

### Q: How long does approval take?
**A:** Usually 1-2 business days. Admins get automatic notifications and can approve quickly.

### Q: What if I'm rejected?
**A:** You'll get an email explaining why. You can contact your admin or submit a new request with more information.

### Q: Can I request multiple signatory names?
**A:** Yes! Submit separate requests for each name you need to sign as.

### Q: What's 2FA and why do I need it?
**A:** Two-Factor Authentication adds security. When you sign, you'll get a code via email to verify it's really you.

### Q: What if I don't get the email code?
**A:** Check your spam folder. If still missing, contact IT support.

### Q: Can I change my signature later?
**A:** Yes! Just click "edit" next to your signature to redraw it.

### Q: What if I forget to sign a report?
**A:** You can sign it later. The system tracks when signatures are applied.
